NEW Water Quality Report

Climate Smart Pilots, within the NSW Department of Primary Industries is trialling a new water quality reporting method. This work is aimed at providing a quick overview of relevant information such as salinity, temperature, precipitation, and upstream flow rates. Data is updated every 6 hours and can be accessed via the links above as either online interactive charts or as a PDF report.
