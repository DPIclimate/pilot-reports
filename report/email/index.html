Recent Flooding

The recent flooding resulted in a significant amount of debris entering the Clyde River. Unfortunately, one of the buoy's located at Angry Man Point was wrapped up in several trees. Thankfully Pearly Oysters was able to remove the buoy and debris from the waterway.
